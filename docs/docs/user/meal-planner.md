---
title: "Meal Planner"
---

The meal planner is a powerful tool that helps you organize your meals for the week.

## Adding Recipes to Your Meal Plan

To add a recipe to your meal plan, follow these steps:

1. Find the recipe you want to add.
2. On the recipe detail screen, tap the "Add to Meal Plan" button.
3. Select the day and meal (breakfast, lunch, or dinner) for which you want to schedule the recipe.

## Viewing Your Meal Plan

You can view your meal plan by tapping on the "Meal Plan" tab. This will show you a calendar view of your scheduled meals for the week. You can tap on any meal to see the recipe details.
