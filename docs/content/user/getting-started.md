This guide will help you get up and running with Zest.

## Installation

Zest is available on the following platforms:

*   **Web:** Access the Zest web app directly from your browser.
*   **iOS:** Download the Zest app from the App Store.
*   **Android:** Download the Zest app from the Google Play Store.

## Creating an Account

To get the most out of Zest, you'll need to create an account. This will allow you to save your favorite recipes, create meal plans, and more.

1.  Open the Zest app or visit the Zest website.
2.  Click on the "Sign Up" button.
3.  Enter your email address and create a password.
4.  Click "Sign Up" to complete the process.

You can also sign up using your Google or Apple account for a faster experience.
